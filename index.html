Welcome to Silly Lena's Eco-Friendly Makerspace

About Me

Hi there! I'm Silly Lena, a 21-year-old eco-enthusiast with a passion for repurposing electronics. My journey is all about exploring hobbies and turning discarded gadgets into treasures.

My Mission

I'm on a mission to open a space for fellow eco-activists where we can teach, learn, and participate in sustainable practices. It's not just about fixing electronics; it's about fostering a community dedicated to the art of recycling and upcycling.

Join the Movement

If you're interested in becoming part of this eco-adventure, join us! We're currently gathering enthusiasts and spreading the word about recycling through our makerspace.
